Pengyu An（安鹏宇）

I am a third-year undergraduate student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I am a member of the Zhiyuan Honor Program. My undergraduate major is Intelligent Sensing Engineering (ISE).

My research interest mainly focuses on robotic manipulation, computer vision (CV) and Large language model (LLM). As for robotic manipulation, I am aimed to develop robots equipped with generalizable, versatile and robust manipulation capabilities. I am also interested in the perception and reconstruction of visual information. Besides, the optimization and acceleration of large language models also fascinates me.

During my undergraduate study, I am fortunate to be mentored by Prof.Yonglu Li in the MVIG-RHOS team.
