Pengyu An（安鹏宇）

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I am a member of the Zhiyuan Engineering Honor Program. My undergraduate major is Intelligent Sensing Engineering (ISE), which is related to AI, control, sensing, information processing and so forth.

My research interest mainly focuses on robotic manipulation and multimodal large language model (MLLM). I am aimed to develop robots equipped with generalizable, versatile and robust manipulation capabilities. I am also interested in the optimization and construction of effective models which can better understand data of different modalities such as vision, tactile, language and so on.

During my undergraduate study, I am fortunate to be mentored by Prof.Yonglu Li in the MVIG-RHOS team.
